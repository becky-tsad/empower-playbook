Essential Implementation Tools

Research-backed tools to support your Empower implementation.

Beacon: The Navigator Data Tool

Purpose-built for economic mobility tracking with AI co-pilot capabilities.

  • AI Co-pilot for Navigators: Intelligent decision support during participant interactions
  • Real-time Meeting Transcription: Automatic documentation of all navigator sessions
  • Participant Portal: Secure document upload and progress tracking for families
  • Family-Level Outcome Tracking: Comprehensive data on economic mobility progress
  • Benefits Cliff Monitoring: Integrated alerts for benefits cliff exposure changes

As we discovered: "If you have the wrong data being captured from the start, your pilot will be confusing and unable to get more funding." Beacon solves the data infrastructure problem that kills programs.

Benefits Cliff Diagnostic Tool

Interactive assessment for communities to understand their benefits cliff landscape.

  • County/Regional Assessment: Determine how many people in your area are in the "cliff zone"
  • Economic Impact Quantification: Understand local costs of benefits cliffs
  • Outreach Starter: Perfect conversation opener with potential partners and funders

Atlanta Fed CLIFF Dashboard

The gold standard for benefits cliff modeling. State-specific calculators to help families visualize income vs. benefits tradeoffs.

Employer ROI Calculator

Calculate your company's return on investment from participating in the Empower Certified Employer program.
