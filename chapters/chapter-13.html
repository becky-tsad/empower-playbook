Essential Implementation Tools

Built from real program experience. Tested with real families. Ready to deploy.

Beacon: The Navigator Data Tool

Built specifically for economic mobility. AI co-pilot included.

  • AI Co-pilot for Navigators: Smart decision support right in the navigator's workflow
  • Real-time Meeting Transcription: Every navigator session documented automatically
  • Family Portal: Families upload documents and track their own progress
  • Family-Level Outcome Tracking: Real data on real economic mobility gains
  • Benefits Cliff Monitoring: Alerts fire when a family's benefits cliff exposure changes

Hard lesson learned: "If you capture the wrong data from the start, your pilot will be confusing and unable to get more funding." Beacon solves that. Get the data right from day one.

Benefits Cliff Diagnostic Tool

See exactly how the benefits cliff hits your community. Interactive. Eye-opening.

  • County/Regional Assessment: Find out how many families in your area are trapped in the "cliff zone"
  • Economic Impact Quantification: Put a dollar figure on what the benefits cliff costs your community
  • Outreach Starter: The best conversation opener you will ever hand a funder or partner

Atlanta Fed CLIFF Dashboard

The gold standard for benefits cliff modeling. State-specific calculators that show families exactly where the cliff hits.
