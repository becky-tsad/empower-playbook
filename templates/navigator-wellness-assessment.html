Navigator Wellness Self-Assessment

Confidential tool for navigators to monitor burnout risk and compassion fatigue

Why This Matters

Navigator work is emotionally demanding. You carry the weight of families' struggles, navigate complex systems, and celebrate hard-won victories. This assessment helps you recognize when you need support—not as a sign of weakness, but as essential maintenance for sustainable impact. Your wellness directly affects the families you serve.

Section 1: Physical Wellness

Rate how often you've experienced these in the past two weeks.

1.1 Difficulty sleeping or changes in sleep patterns

1.2 Headaches, muscle tension, or physical exhaustion

1.3 Changes in appetite or eating habits

1.4 Getting sick more often than usual

Section 2: Emotional Wellness

Rate how often you've experienced these in the past two weeks.

2.1 Feeling emotionally drained at the end of the day

2.2 Feeling overwhelmed or unable to cope

2.3 Irritability or frustration with participants or colleagues

2.4 Difficulty feeling hopeful about participants' futures

2.5 Thinking about work during off-hours or difficulty "switching off"

Section 3: Professional Engagement

Rate how often you've experienced these in the past two weeks.

3.1 Difficulty staying focused during participant meetings

3.2 Feeling detached or numb when hearing participants' challenges

3.3 Questioning whether this work makes a difference

3.4 Dreading going to work or avoiding certain participants

Section 4: Support Systems

These protective factors reduce burnout risk. Rate your current access.

4.1 I have regular supervision/support from my manager

4.2 I can talk to colleagues who understand this work

4.3 I have activities outside work that help me recharge

4.4 My caseload is manageable (≤25 families)

Understanding Your Results

Low Risk (Burnout 0-17, Protective 12-16)

You're managing well. Continue your self-care practices and support system engagement. Monitor any changes.

Moderate Risk (Burnout 18-34 OR Protective 8-11)

Warning signs present. Schedule time with your supervisor to discuss workload. Prioritize self-care and peer support. Consider which protective factors you can strengthen.

High Risk (Burnout 35-52 OR Protective 0-7)

Immediate attention needed. Please speak with your supervisor this week about caseload adjustment and support needs. This is not a failure—it's the work recognizing you need care too.
