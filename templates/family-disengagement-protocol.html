Family Disengagement Protocol

Standard procedures for when families reduce or stop engagement

Our Philosophy on Disengagement

Disengagement is not failure—it's information. Families disengage for many valid reasons: life crises, changing circumstances, the program not meeting their needs, or readiness timing. Our role is to:

  • Understand the reason without judgment
  • Honor family autonomy and self-determination
  • Keep the door open for re-engagement
  • Learn from patterns to improve the program
