Transportation Barrier Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of transportation challenges for rural families

Transportation: THE Barrier in Rural Areas

In rural communities, transportation isn't just a barrier—it's often THE barrier that makes or breaks economic mobility. Without reliable transportation:

  • Families can't access jobs, especially those with non-traditional hours
  • Childcare drop-off/pick-up becomes impossible
  • Training programs and education are out of reach
  • Healthcare appointments are missed
  • Benefits recertification appointments are missed (triggering loss of benefits)

Average rural commute in Tennessee: 35+ minutes one-way. Average taxi/rideshare cost: Not available in most rural counties.
