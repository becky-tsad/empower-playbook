Calculation Methodology

Turnover Cost: Employees x Turnover Rate x Cost Per Hire

Productivity Loss: Financially Stressed Employees x $3,000/year (research-backed average)

Absenteeism Cost: Financially Stressed Employees x 3.5 extra days x Hourly Wage x 8 hours

Program Investment: $500 per financially stressed employee (comprehensive support estimate)

Savings Projections: 25% turnover reduction, 15% productivity improvement, 15% absenteeism reduction (conservative estimates)
